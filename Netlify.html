Netlify Website Deployment Guide

Sign Up on Netlify:

  1. Visit the Netlify website.
  2. Click on the "Sign Up" button.
  3. Enter your email address and create a password.
  4. Complete the sign-up process by following the on-screen instructions.

Login to Your Netlify Account:

  1. Go to the Netlify website.
  2. Click on the "Log In" button.
  3. Enter your email address and password.
  4. Click on the "Log In" button to access your account.

Deploy Your Website on Netlify:

  1. Once logged in, click on the "New Site from Git" button.
  2. Choose your Git provider (e.g., GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket) and select your repository.
  3. Configure your build settings (if necessary).
  4. Click on the "Deploy Site" button to deploy your website.
  5. Wait for the deployment process to complete.
  6. Access your live website using the provided URL.
