List

  1. ol = Ordered List:
    2. Ordered lists are lists where each item in the list is numbered.
    Example:
    1. Ameenullah
    2. Abrar
    3. Waleed
  2. ul = Unordered List:
    3. Unordered lists are lists that begin with a bullet point.
    Example:
    • Adnan
    • Ahmad
    • Amjad
  3. dl = Define List:
    4. Definition lists are made up of a set of terms along with the definitions for each of those terms.
    Example:
    Science:
    Science is the systematic study of the natural world through observation, experimentation, and analysis.
    Math:
    Mathematics is the study of numbers, quantities, shapes, and patterns, and the relationships between them.
